Scoring After a player goes out, the round has ended, and scores are tallied as follows: Red books are worth 500 points Black books are worth 300 points Jokers are worth 50 points 2s are worth 20 points Aces are worth 20 points 9s through Kings are worth 10 points 4s through 8s are worth 5 points Going out is worth 100 points Points from the cards in the team’s remaining hand are deducted from the points from the team’s played cards.
